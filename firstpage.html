Inception (2010)

PG-13 | 148 min | Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi, Thriller

Synopsis: From the filmmaker behind 'The Dark Knight' and 'Memento', comes this gripping, wildly original thriller starring Leonardo DiCaprio as Cobb, the head of a criminal ring that specializes in a very unique type of corporate espionage: they invade the dreams of their victims to steal ideas. When he is asked to do something that has never been done -plant an idea in the mind of a young man that will eventually dissolve his future empire- the increasingly unstable Cobb sees it as his last chance to escape his life of exile. But to achieve "INCEPTION", Cobb and his team must navigate a seemingly impossible maze in an ever-collapsing world of dreams.

Director & Writer: Christopher Nolan
